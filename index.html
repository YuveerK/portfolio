Portfolio

Projects I've worked on

The RedApp is a tele-medicine application that allows patients to have a virtual consultation with doctors from anywhere. The system allows doctors to create and view records of patients as well as prescribe any medication after the consultation. The system has all the features necessary that a patient and doctor will require at a real hospital. The RedApp has systems in place to capture patients vital signs, create sicknotes for patients, upload patient reports, book consultations, view patients history. There are 2 systems that were developed. A patients side as well as a doctors side. A patient can view all his / her records and vitals in the form of graphs.
